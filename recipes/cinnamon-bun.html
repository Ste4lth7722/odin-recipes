Cinnamon Bun

Description

Soft, sweet cinnamon buns topped with a simple glaze — perfect for breakfast or dessert.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups (440 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (240 ml) warm milk
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1/4 cup (50 g) sugar
  • 1/4 cup (60 g) melted butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • For filling: 1/2 cup (100 g) brown sugar, 2 tbsp ground cinnamon, 3 tbsp softened butter
  • For glaze: 1 cup powdered sugar, 2 tbsp milk, 1/2 tsp vanilla (optional)

Steps

  1. Bloom yeast: mix warm milk, sugar, and yeast; let sit 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Mix in melted butter and egg. Gradually add flour and salt, knead until smooth and elastic (about 8 minutes).
  3. Place dough in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise 1-1½ hours until doubled.
  4. Roll dough into a rectangle, spread softened butter, sprinkle brown sugar and cinnamon evenly, then roll tightly from the long side.
  5. Cut into 12 rolls, place in a greased pan, cover, and let rise 30-45 minutes.
  6. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake rolls 18-22 minutes until golden.
  7. Whisk glaze ingredients and drizzle over warm buns before serving.
