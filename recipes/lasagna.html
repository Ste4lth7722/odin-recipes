Lasagna

Description

A classic baked lasagna with a rich meat sauce and layers of cheesy ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles (or enough for 3 layers)
  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz (680 g) marinara or tomato sauce
  • 15 oz (425 g) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups (200 g) shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup (50 g) grated Parmesan
  • 1 egg (optional, for ricotta mixture)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano or Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook lasagna noodles according to package; drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté onion until translucent; add garlic and cook 1 minute.
  3. Add ground beef, cook until browned; drain excess fat. Stir in tomato sauce and seasonings; simmer 10 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg (if using), half the Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a thin layer of sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat for 3 layers, finishing with sauce and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  6. Cover with foil and bake 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake 10-15 minutes more until bubbly and golden.
  7. Let rest 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
