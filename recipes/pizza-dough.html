Pizza Dough

Description

If you're looking for a homemade pizza crust recipe that's great for beginners, you're in luck. This top-rated recipe is super easy to throw together on a whim - and it puts the store-bought stuff to shame. Learn how to make the best pizza crust of your life with just a few ingredients, find out how to shape the dough, and get our best storage secrets.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups (440 g) all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
  • 1 cup (240 ml) warm water (about 110°F / 43°C)
  • 2 1/4 tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. In a bowl, dissolve the sugar and yeast in warm water; let sit 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Stir in the olive oil, then gradually add flour and salt until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Turn dough onto a floured surface and knead 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise 1-1½ hours until doubled.
  5. Punch down, divide, shape, and let rest 15-30 minutes before stretching into crusts.
  6. Preheat oven (and pizza stone if using) to 475-500°F (245-260°C). Top as desired and bake 8-12 minutes.
